    Evaporating and Condensing Tank

    The main function of this system is to concentrate the desired product, evaporate the solvent used, and recover the solvent by condensing the vapor.

    Liquid Liquid Tank

    This equipment will be used for liquid-liquid extraction using organic solvents to obtain the product from the production medium. The tank will have capacity of 100 L.

    Storage Tank

    This tank serves as the storage tank for solvents.

    Decontamination Tank

    This tank used for decontamination of spent media. Decontamination will be done at 121 celsius and 15 psig.

    Bioreactor Tank

    This tank will serve as the main bacteria incubation tank. All parts of the tank should be airtight and can withstand a pressure of 15psig. The culture media will also be mixed and sterilized in this tank. Bacteria will be inoculated for 16-hours. Since the process will be aerobic, air will be supplied through the media.
